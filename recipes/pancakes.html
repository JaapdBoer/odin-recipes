Pancakes

Pancakes are easy and dilicious to make. Try this recipe if you are in need of a quick breakfast!

For this recipe you will need just some basic kitchen utensils and a few common ingredients. Read on to see for yourself.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of flour
  • 100ml of milk
  • 1 egg
  • some butter for baking

Steps

  1. Put the flour in a bowl
  2. Add the egg and milk
  3. Stir from the center of the bowl to the outside of the bowl until your batter is homonegized.
  4. Use just enough butter in a bakingpan to coat it entirely.
  5. Pour in the batter and leave it undesturped till the top is cooked.
  6. Flip the pancake on the other side and bake until goldenbrown.
  7. Enjoy!
