Pita Bread

Pita bread is an easy recipe to make at home and will be way better then the storebought equicalent

Ingredients

  • 500gr flour
  • 400gr lukewarm water
  • 25gr olive oil
  • 1 package (or 7gr) yeast
  • 2 gr salt
  • 30gr spices
  • flour for dusting

Steps

  1. Add the water, yeast, olive oil and spices to the flour in a mixingbowl.
  2. Combine everything till a ragged ball of dough forms. Then add the salt.
  3. Knead for 20 minutes by hand or 10 minutes in a standmixer.
  4. Let the dough rest for 2 hours in a bowl with a damp towel on top.
  5. After 1 hour and 30 minutes pre heat the oven on the highest temperature possible.
  6. After 2 hours deflate the dough and devide it in 8 equal pieces.
  7. Shape the pieces by hand or with a rollingpin.
  8. Bake the pieces for 2-5 minutes in the oven. This depends on your oven so keep an eye on them while they bake.
  9. Enjoy!
